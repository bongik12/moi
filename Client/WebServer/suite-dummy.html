Trading Engine

The trading engine hierarchy is the data structure used by the trading bot to keep runtime information highly accessible and exposed to others.

You will use this hierarchy for two main purposes:

The hierarchy exposes—literally—all the information processed by the trading bot, providing a comprehensive feedback loop to trading systems and analysis capabilities to users.   • To keep track of the actions: By analyzing runtime information, you may gain a detailed understanding of what happens, when, and why, throughout a trading session.
